iOS Sideloading

iOS Sideloading is the when you install apps on an Apple device through other means than the apps store. I have tried Sideloadly. It is very simple to use, and I installed the Onion Browser! The IPA for it are here.

Sideloadly is far from the only option, and AltStore is a very popular way to sideload apps! AltStore requires you to sync to a computer weekly, which is why forks like SideStore exist!
