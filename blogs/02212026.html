Switching from AltStore to SideStore

Recently, AltStore users would of gotten a error code -22410 when trying to refresh apps using AltServer on Windows. After waiting days for altserver 1.7.3 to release, I tried to refresh my apps, but I kept getting that error. So... I switched to SideStore. SideStore is a fork of AltStore, by the community, due to AltStores lack of development. The most notable feature is being able to refresh apps without a computer, using LocalDevVPN, but another feature I really like is the official support for linux. I would strongly recommend SideStore over Altstore!

FYI do not update to iOS 26.4, it currently breaks SideStore.
