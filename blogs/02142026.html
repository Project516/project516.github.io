Switching from ALtStore to SideStore

Recently, AltStore users would of gotten a error code -22410 when trying to refresh apps using altserver on windows. After waiting days for altserver 1.7.3 to release, I tried to refresh my apps, but I kept getting that error. So... I switched to SideStore. SideStore is a fork of AltStore, by the community, due to AltStores lack of development. The most notable feature is being able to refresh apps without a computer, using LocalDevVPN, but another feature I really like is the offical support for linux.
